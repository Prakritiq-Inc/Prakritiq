Our first product

Indoor CO₂ Reduction Device (In Development)

Our first product focuses on reducing indoor CO₂ concentration in enclosed environments. The goal is to improve air quality without infrastructure-heavy changes or constant user intervention.

The product is currently in early development, with technical feasibility under exploration and an MVP target of approximately 12 months.

Designed for adoption, not enforcement

Most indoor air quality solutions depend on increased ventilation, high energy usage, or large infrastructure changes.

Prakritiq’s approach prioritizes seamless coexistence with existing systems, minimal maintenance, and adoption by default.

Initial focus environments

Offices, educational institutions, enclosed professional environments, and research or lab-adjacent spaces.

These settings face clear indoor air quality challenges and are well-positioned to adopt early-stage sustainability technologies.

Beyond a single product

The indoor CO₂ device is only the starting point.

Prakritiq’s long-term vision is to build a portfolio of sustainability technologies — each addressing a specific friction point that prevents adoption.
