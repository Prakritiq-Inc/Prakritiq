Sustainability, designed into systems — not added later.

Prakritiq builds sustainability technologies that remove operational friction, making adoption inevitable rather than forced.

What we believe

Sustainability has been framed as responsibility, compliance, or optics. We reject that framing.

Systems should be designed such that sustainability is a natural outcome — not a burden carried after profits are made.

What we are building

Our first product is an indoor CO₂ reduction device focused on enclosed, high-occupancy environments.

The goal is simple: improve indoor conditions without operational disruption, behavior enforcement, or complex infrastructure changes.

Status

The product is currently in early development. We are working toward a functional MVP within the next year.
